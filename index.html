PupSocial connects proud dog parents and their pups in the area

About

PupSocial platform has been created for all dog owners and their pups to unite, make new friends and relationships, and connect with people who share the same interest – a love for dogs. The app facilitates communication between dog parents in your area. Whether you have a question, need advice from another parent, or want to meet in real life and establish new meaningful relationships, this app is the perfect tool to do it all.

The cause we care deeply about
While we all have fun here, we are aware that some dogs are less fortunate than others. Having witnessed homeless and hungry dogs firsthand, we commit ourselves to help minimize this horrific problem in less well-off and less-regulated countries. We believe that every dog deserves to have a place to live, eat, and feel loved. This is why part of our profit will go to save a dog, one life at a time.

Guidelines

1. When registering, for the time being our app only uses Facebook. We will never post anything on your behalf. We use Facebook to make your experience faster and secure.

2. Create a profile for you and your pup

3. Swipe through the pictures to look for dog owners and their dogs near you.

4. Found someone you want to hang out with? Send them an invite to go for a walk. If your invitation is accepted, you will be able to chat with them and arrange the details. The invite will expire in 24 hours if it isn’t accepted.

5. Like someone and would like to stay in touch? Send a friend request and chat inside the app.

6. ave a party or special occasion for a gathering coming up? Create a meet up group… and let's all have fun!
